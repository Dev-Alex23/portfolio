Photography Art

The web application was developed for a Photography company to manage their business operations. Visitors have the ability to see necessary information about what the company offers, such as timetables for classes and upcoming exhibits with the option to join and, if they register with the company, they will be able to book the different events.

The manager also has the ability create, update and view reports about events such as the total revenue generated by an exhibit, lecturer or see comments on classes held. If an artist application is accepted, they can now upload evidence of their artwork and have registered users comment on them.
